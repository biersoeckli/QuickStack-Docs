2FA adds a time-based one-time code (TOTP) requirement at login, in addition to your password. Supported by any TOTP app:

- Bitwarden
- Google Authenticator
- Microsoft Authenticator
- Authy
- 1Password

## Enabling 2FA

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**.
2. Locate the **2FA Settings** card and click **Enable 2FA**.

<img src="/img/docs/manage-quickstack-cluster/two-factor-authentication/2fa-step-1.png" alt="2FA Settings Card" />

3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app.

<img src="/img/docs/manage-quickstack-cluster/two-factor-authentication/2fa-step-2.png" alt="QR Code Dialog" />

4. Enter the 6-digit code from your app and click **Verify**.

2FA is now active for your account.

## Replacing 2FA (new device or app)

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**.
2. Click **Replace current 2FA Config**.
3. Scan the new QR code and verify.

The old configuration is immediately invalidated.

## Disabling 2FA

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**.
2. Click **Disable 2FA** and confirm with your current 2FA code.
